St. Xavier’s Senior Secondary School:

Administered by the Jesuits, St. Xavier’s Senior Secondary School is a co-educational day school affiliated with the CBSE. It was founded in 1941 under the name of St. Mary’s Boys’ School. But it was later renamed to St. Xavier’s Secondary School in 1945.

India’s International School:

St. Angela Sophia Senior Secondary School:

St. Angela Sophia Senior Secondary School is an all-girls day school managed by the Mission Sisters of Ajmer. It was established as a Boarding House in Ajmer to care for Christian orphans. The orphanage was shifted to Jaipur as a school. .

Rajasthan Open State School:

Rajasthan Open State School is a government institution that offers high-quality education through open schooling method. It aims to educate children of remote and rural areas. For class X, the students have to select five out of 15 subjects, and for class XII, the students have to choose five out of twenty subjects.
