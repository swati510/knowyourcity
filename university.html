UNIVERSITY OF RAJASTHAN

Established in 1947, University of Rajasthan is a non-profit public higher education institution located in the urban setting of the large city of Jaipur (population range of 1,000,000-5,000,000 inhabitants), Rajasthan. Officially accredited and/or recognized by the University Grants Commission, India, University of Rajasthan is a coeducational higher education institution. University of Rajasthan offers courses and programs leading to officially recognized higher education degrees such as bachelor degrees in several areas of study.
