SHOPPING CENTRES

Johari Bazaar

Yes, we know you've heard of it! But coming to Jaipur and not shopping at Johri bazaar would be sacrilege. From gold and silver jewellery to traditional kundan jewellery you can find it all here. While you’re there you can also buy traditional Rajasthani sarees and lehengas. That’s all of Rajasthan in one market, make the most of it.

What to buy: Kundan Jewellery

Getting there

Bapu Bazaar

Bapu Bazaar is a great place to stock up on embroidered garments This is the fashion street of Jaipur. With a magnificent collection of skirts, leheria stoles and special Jaipuri woolens and shawls, this place is where you can get the best deals on the prettiest things. Bapu Bazaar also has many shops selling Lac jewellery- a must buy from Rajasthan.

What to buy: Lac Bangles

Getting there

Badi Chaupar

Badi Chaupar-A perfect place to buy gifts for folks back home Shopping in Jaipur has to include shopping at Badi Chaupar. If you're in a hurry and need to shop fast, this is your go-to place. From block printed clothes to traditional souvenirs and gift items, you will find everything here.

What to buy: Embroidered bed sheets

Getting there

Nehru Bazaar

The best place to buy Mojris in Jaipur Another important shopping destination in Jaipur is Nehru Bazaar. If you’re looking at buying beautiful Rajasthani mojris then this is the place to go. There are mojris in different colours and styles and you’ll be spoilt for choice. Nehru Bazaar also has hand printed dress material and bandhani dupattas at affordable prices.

What to buy: Mojris

Getting there

Chaura Rasta

Your best bet for all things stationary Another thing that Jaipur is well known for is the variety of fine hand-printed paper. Chaura Rasta is a delight for stationery lovers. You’ll find a wide assortment of papers and books at affordable prices.

What to buy: Handmade paper diaries

Getting there

Gaurav Towers

Don't be misled by the notion that just because this is a mall it will have overpriced clothes and accessories. Gaurav Towers has the best of everything all under one roof. Right from brands like Provogue, Bata, Woodland and Levis. Apart from that there are many other stores selling designer clothes, ethnic wear and accessories. This is also a great place to stock up on cosmetics. There are also many restaurants where you can sample delicious chaat, dosas and rolls.

What to buy: Handbags and cosmetics

Getting there

Blue Pottery

You’ll be able to spot stunning pots, jars and vessels painted in blue hues, famously known as blue pottery, across most bazaars in Jaipur. But if you’re looking to buy blue pottery collectables from specific stores, head to Neerja (Bhawani Singh Road, C-Scheme Extn. Jacob Road, Civil Lines), Kripal Kumbh, Heritage India (38, Golimar Garden Scheme) and Bharat Potteries (Road no. 6, VKI Area)

What to buy: ashtrays, pots, jars, vessels

Getting there
